ABOUT US

Our founders have come together from India, UK, Germany and the USA to invest their extensive global experience in the FinTech industry, and create a leading brand that transforms tax management and filing. Our team is dedicated to making sure that all your tax and finance related regulations and compliances are met and updated effortlessly. We focus on simplifying the tedious process of taxation and help you navigate through it step by step. 
Vision

To stand out and be a leading innovative product company in finance and tax technology domain in every market we operate in.

Mission

Shaping the future of finance and tax through our bleeding edge technology, unprecedented values and by creating opportunities for our customers, employees and ecosystem.

OUR STORY

Our logo symbolizes professionalism in the services we offer. It embodies the rise of artificial intelligence out of the learning and knowledge we have accumulated over the years. It shows the active combination of artificial intelligence and machine learning to improve and secure the block chain system. Our name is made up of two words – TAX, which represents our domain of expertise and GENIE which signifies the ease of use of our platform. These are the qualities that we take pride in and never compromise.
